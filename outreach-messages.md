## 💡 COLD OUTREACH TIPS

1. **Personalize every message** — Mention something specific about their site
2. **Lead with value** — Don't just sell, show what's broken and how you'll fix it
3. **Use social proof** — Link to your portfolio constantly
4. **Create urgency** — "Only taking 10 clients this quarter"
5. **Make it easy** — Provide calendar link, don't make them chase you
6. **Follow up 3 times** — Most sales happen after the 5th touchpoint
7. **A/B test subject lines** — Track what gets opened
8. **Best sending times** — Tuesday/Wednesday 10am-11am or 2pm-3pm
9. **Mobile-optimize** — Most people read emails on phones
10. **End with clear CTA** — Always tell them exactly what to do next
